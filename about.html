About Us
We are two brothers with a love for chess

When our grandfather first taught the two of us to play chess we were intantly hooked. Something about the intricacies of how the pieces moved along side the strong strategic component of the game was very addicting.

As we grew older we both came to realize just how crucial it is to have a solid understanding of fundmental chess strategy and tactics.

When you make a mistake in chess you have no one to blame but yourself. The consequences of careless play immediatly show up on the board as a lost piece or a positional disadvantage. When we lose a game of chess we can be sure that we made a mistake many moves ago.

Much like in life, we must think about each of our steps very carefully, avoiding pitfals, and keeping the end in mind. This is why we both have such a strong love for the game and an even greater passion for teaching it to the Armenian youth.
